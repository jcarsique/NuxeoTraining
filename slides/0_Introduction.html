### Contributors - Sun TAN - Arnaud Kervern - Alain Escaffre - Mariana Cedica - Thomas Roger
### Nuxeo – Open Source ECM - Founded in 2000, 500+ customers, 5000+ installations  - ~ 50 people in the company, 70% engineers, great place to work - Dual HQ Paris & Boston, VC backed - Global Partner Network - Open Source Enterprise Content Management provider with global install base - On the forefront of innovation, with a dedicated research lab contributing to several industry initiatives, consortiums and Open Source foundations - Nuxeo Connect subscription offering for maintenance, support, and access to - Nuxeo Studio - Nuxeo Marketplace
